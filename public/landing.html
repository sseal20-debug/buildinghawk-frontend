FAQ

Common Questions

What is BuildingHawk?▼
BuildingHawk is a Visual Property Intelligence platform for commercial real estate. It combines 4K aerial imagery, automated building data extraction, interactive map overlays, contact management, due diligence workflows, and one-click report generation into one visual system — replacing spreadsheets, static PDFs, and disconnected CRE tools.
What does "Visual Property Intelligence" mean?▼
Visual Property Intelligence is the core architecture of BuildingHawk. Every building data point — clear height, dock doors, power specs, zoning, environmental status — is tied to visual context: aerial imagery, satellite views, orthomosaic maps, and 3D models. You see the data on the property, not in a spreadsheet. Every report, brochure, and evaluation flows through this visual layer.
What building specs does BuildingHawk track?▼
Full industrial spec sheet: clear height, dock-high and ground-level door counts, power distribution (120/208V and 277/480V systems, amperage and voltage), total SF, truckwell dimensions, office-to-warehouse ratio, freeway frontage, signage, and zoning. Building types include distribution, warehouse, office/warehouse, freestanding industrial, manufacturing, and office.
How accurate is the aerial imagery?▼
BuildingHawk captures 4K aerial imagery processed into orthomosaic maps at 1–2 cm/pixel GSD (Ground Sample Distance). 3D photogrammetric modeling achieves sub-2 cm lidar-grade accuracy. All flight operations are FAA Part 107 certified with full compliance documentation.
What markets does BuildingHawk cover?▼
Orange County (Anaheim, Fullerton, Brea, Irvine, Santa Ana, Garden Grove, Buena Park, Yorba Linda, Placentia), Inland Empire (Corona, Ontario, Chino, Chino Hills), Santa Fe Springs and Norwalk, and the greater Southern California industrial markets along the 91, 57, 55, and 22 freeway corridors.
Can BuildingHawk replace my CRM and spreadsheets?▼
BuildingHawk replaces Excel, Google Sheets, shared drives, and generic CRMs that don't understand building data. Every contact links to a property. Every property links to its deals, documents, and specs. Every record includes visual context — aerial imagery, maps, and condition data. It's a single system where everything connects visually.
Does BuildingHawk integrate with GIS and CoStar?▼
BuildingHawk supports Esri ArcGIS compatible data export, CoStar data overlay for market comparables, and GeoJSON/KML export for third-party GIS applications. Zoning, environmental, and title data layers can be overlaid directly on the aerial map view.
